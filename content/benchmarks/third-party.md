## Evaluation Sources

### LMSYS Chatbot Arena
Community-based comparison platform with human preference ratings.

### HELM (Holistic Evaluation of Language Models)  
Comprehensive academic evaluation framework from Stanford.

### MMLU (Massive Multitask Language Understanding)
Broad coverage of subject matter expertise across 57 tasks.

### Big-Bench
Collaborative benchmark suite with diverse evaluation tasks.

## Methodology

Third-party results are aggregated from published reports and academic papers. Performance metrics represent normalized scores across different evaluation frameworks.
