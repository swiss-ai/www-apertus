## Contributing

We are interested in your results! Please send us links to publications where you evaluate our models, such as reports on:

- **Common sense reasoning:** everyday knowledge, logical inference, and practical understanding.
- **Mathematical problem solving:** evaluates arithmetic, algebra, and quantitative reasoning skills.
- **Code generation tasks:** assess programming ability, algorithm implementation, and debugging.
- **Natural language understanding** to measure reading comprehension, linguistic analysis, and semantic understanding.

All models should be evaluated using standardized test sets with identical evaluation criteria. Performance metrics represent accuracy percentages across multiple test instances. Additional guidelines will follow soon.
