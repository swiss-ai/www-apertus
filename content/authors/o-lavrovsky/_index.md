Data engineer and developer relations specialist with deep open-source technology expertise. Background in startups, academia & industry, contributions to a range of technical projects like [Dribdat](https://dribdat.cc), [CKAN](https://opendata.swiss), [Django](https://www.djangoproject.com/), [Proxeus](https://proxeus.org) & [Frictionless Data](https://frictionlessdata.io). Prior to joining the [Swiss AI Initiative](https://www.swiss-ai.org/) and the [EPFL AI Centre](https://ai.epfl.ch), O lectured at HSLU in the [Data Design + Art](https://www.hslu.ch/de-ch/design-film-kunst/studium/bachelor/data-design-and-art/) program, and continues to support teaching and research collaborations with universities and centres of excellence around Switzerland. A student in [Communications Systems](https://www.epfl.ch/education/bachelor/programs/communication-systems/) from 2003, who shares stories of a life-long fascination with protocols, networks and signals at [log.alets.ch](https://log.alets.ch)
