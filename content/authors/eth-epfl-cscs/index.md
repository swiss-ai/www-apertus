---
title: "ETH, EPFL and CSCS"
---

The Swiss AI Initiative, started in December 2023, is the largest open science/open source effort for AI foundation models worldwide, and the first initiative of the [Swiss National AI Institute](https://ethz.ch/en/news-and-events/eth-news/news/2024/10/eth-zurich-and-epfl-enhance-collaboration-to-boost-ai-in-switzerland.html), a partnership between the [ETH AI Center](https://ai.ethz.ch/) and the [EPFL AI Center](https://ai.epfl.ch/). The initiative further benefits from the critical mass of expertise of over 800 researchers (including 70 AI-focused professors) from over 10 academic institutions across Switzerland. The frontier AI research is enabled by one of the world's leading AI supercomputer "[Alps](https://www.cscs.ch/computers/alps)" (with over 10'000 GH200 GPUs) in collaboration with engineers at the [Swiss National Supercomputing Centre (CSCS)](https://www.cscs.ch/)[.](https://www.cscs.ch/) Regular compute calls are available to bring together researchers from different organizations.The initiative provides artifacts such as transparent and open software, models, and data releases, enabling their trustworthy use by various Swiss stakeholders, including SMEs and start-ups.

For more information, visit [swiss-ai.org](https://www.swiss-ai.org)
