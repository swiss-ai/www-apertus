<p class="section-intro">Common questions about Apertus and the Swiss AI Initiative.</p>

<div class="faq-item">
<h4>What makes Apertus different from other open models?</h4>
<p>Apertus is fully open — not just the weights, but the complete training data, code, methods, and alignment principles are documented and accessible.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h4>Is Apertus compliant with the EU AI Act?</h4>
<p>Yes. Apertus is built to meet EU AI Act requirements from day one, including opt-out mechanisms, PII removal, and memorization prevention.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h4>Can I use Apertus commercially?</h4>
<p>Yes. Apertus is released under Apache 2.0, allowing both research and commercial use.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h4>What languages does Apertus support?</h4>
<p>Apertus is trained on 1000+ languages, making it a strong foundation for further development. Currently, Apertus is conversational in a few dozen languages.</p>
</div>
